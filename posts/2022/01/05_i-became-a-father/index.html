My first week as a father - Life in Japan

  2022-01-05  -    Voylin's Life, Our Life

Voylin became a father

I won’t make any sketches of how I became a father, would be a bit too graphical. But in this video I want to show my last couple of day’s before I became a father and my first day’s of being a father.

It was a very stressful period as soo many things went differently then expected, especially that after my baby was born, he had to go to a different hospital and was separated from the mother for his first week alive. Luckily I could visit him every day, was difficult to go that far by bicycle every since day, but it was worth it to hold him in my arms … he kept sleeping the whole time but it was still worth it. ^^
