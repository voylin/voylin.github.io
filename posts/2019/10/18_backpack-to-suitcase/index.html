From Backpacker to ... Suitcaser?

  2019-10-18  -    Voylin's Life, Travel Advice

I hate suitcases

I really do, even though it is helpful to have so much packing space, it just feels less convenient and I really dislike the inconvenience which it causes every time when I have to use a suitcase. I prefer a backpack which I can easily carry everywhere. With a backpack I know I have all important things with me, but with a suitcase I always feel like I’m forgetting something.

However, there is a place and a time for everything and sometimes the best option is a suitcase. Especially when you are planning on moving to Japan, it can be helpful to take a suitcase of the last ’trip’ before your next visit as your next visit will be for some years, possibly a lifetime.
