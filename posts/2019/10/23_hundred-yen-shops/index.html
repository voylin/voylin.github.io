My opinion of 100円 stores + Building a Samurai

  2019-10-23  -    Voylin's Life

100 jpy stores

They are everywhere in Japan and even though they are convenient, they are not always the cheapest option. If you use a lot of paper and files, try your local supermarket here in Japan if they also have a stationary section. You’ll find prices which are lower or equal, but with better quality. I like browsing through them, but it takes some getting used to before you know if something is a buy or not.
