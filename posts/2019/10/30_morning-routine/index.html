My morning study routine

I’m a real morning person, most day’s I open my eyes at 5 o’clock and I’m fully awake ready to start my day. The only problem I’m facing is my old friend the sofa … He often tempts me to keep sitting there and watching dumb shows which I don’t really care about. ^^"

It took me some years to get enough discipline to actually wake up and study in the morning. On sunny and rainy days, the studying is not a problem. But cloudy days really get me down. Rain is fine, cloudy isn’t and I have no idea why. Well, I hope my morning study routine motivates you as well to start picking up a morning routine. ;)
