Lingodeer has a reasonable price for now!

  2019-12-01  -    Voylin's Life, Learn Japanese

Lingodeer pricing

The pricing of Lingodeer has been a bit all over the place, first free, then donations, then very high prices. Luckily every now and then they have offers (still expensive in my opinion) which makes the price more reasonable.

I myself have Lingodeer lifetime and I’m glad I have it … but I haven’t used it after I got it. The Japanese lessons are … great … but not that useful for my level. However, I am planning on actually using Lingodeer once I get the JLPT N2 or N1. Because after my Japanese I would like to start studying Korean.
