Why I stopped using Memrise

Honestly, I have always had a love hate relationship with Memrise, but like in any relationship, you avoid to look at the negatives until they start becoming too painful. I won’t say that Memrise is a bad tool, but it’s a tool which should be used in a certain way and it is not sufficient for my style of studying anymore.

I’ll probably go back to Memrise in the future, not certain when but I will at some point. ^^ I still have many more languages I want to learn but first I need to finally master Japanese!
