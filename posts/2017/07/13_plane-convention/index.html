Fly-In Moorsele

Small video of my trip to a plane and fly convention near my city. I’ve always been quite interested in planes and everything that flies (like birds :p).

Fly-In Moorsele is an exciting experience for aviation enthusiasts. This annual event, held in the small Belgian town of Moorsele, brings together pilots and aviation fans from all over Belgium to showcase their skills, knowledge and share their love of flying and planes (and everything else which flies with an engine).

The airshow

The centerpiece of Fly-In Moorsele is the airshow, which features a variety of aircraft, including vintage warbirds, aerobatic planes, and modern jets. The pilots put on an impressive display of aerial maneuvers, including loops, rolls, and formation flying. The highlight of the show is the “Missing Man” formation, a tribute to fallen pilots, which is performed by a group of vintage warbirds.

Planes to look at

In addition to the airshow, Fly-In Moorsele also features a static display of aircraft. This is an opportunity to get up close and personal with the planes and learn more about their history and technical specifications. Visitors can also meet the pilots and ask them questions about their aircraft and flying experiences. Some pilots even allow people to enter their aircraft’s to go for a flight.
