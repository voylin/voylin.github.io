Lost in a Forest Semoine

  2017-06-01  -    Voylin's Life

Just a random vlog

This is just a random vlog from my time in Semoine when I was there with my family in our mobile home. It was a nice relaxing trip (except for the kayaking in a river filled with greenery). I decided to go for a solo hike and tried to get lost to have some fun.

I’ve always liked hiking, but because of the speed at which I walk, my family can’t easily keep up so I go alone. My main reason for trying to get lost every time that I hike is because when you get lost, you always find nice things, or at least most of the time.

The benefits of hiking

Hiking is a great way to get outside, exercise, and enjoy nature. Some fresh air every now and then can do wonders to your body and mental health. But, it can also be a way to challenge yourself, push your limits, and learn new things.

One of the most rewarding aspects of hiking is the sense of adventure and discovery that comes with it. On one of my other adventures of getting lost whilst hiking, we stopped at the side of the road and wandered into a forest where we found cars completely consumed by tree’s. Cars which have probably been there since World war 1 or 2. The way that nature had reclaimed those vehicles was quite wonderful to see and showed the strength of nature.

Hiking is a great way to get some exercise and fresh air. It’s a low-impact activity that can be enjoyed by people of all ages and fitness levels. Hiking also has a number of mental health benefits, including reducing stress, anxiety, and depression. Additionally, hiking can help you connect with nature, and it allows you to explore new places and discover new things.

Another good point: It’s cheap! You only need a way to get somewhere and some good hiking shoes. Believe me when I say that a good pair of shoes can make your hiking much more enjoyable and less painful, or even not painful at all. Since I’ve gotten decent hiking shoes, I don’t have pain in my legs or feet anymore, that could also just be because I have been exercising more recently.

Insects and plants

As a plant and insect lover, going on hikes give me opportunities to find new kinds of plant and insect species. Especially ants are something I am very interested in. There are thousands of different species to discover. I like to learn about these things and hiking gives me a chance to review the things I learned and see if I can recognize the plants and different animals which I see.

Embracing the unknown

One of the most exciting things about hiking is the sense of adventure and discovery that comes with it. While it’s important to be prepared and stay on designated trails, getting lost can also be a positive experience. It can be a chance to push yourself out of your comfort zone, and test your problem-solving skills. It also allows you to see new things and make unexpected discoveries.

But before departing on such a getting lost adventure, pack enough water. On one of my hikes I forgot to bring enough water and it was really challenging to keep going. Luckily there were some springs nearby and I just hoped that I wouldn’t get sick. Luckily I didn’t get sick, still don’t just drink from any stream of water. ^^"

Safety

While hiking can be a lot of fun, it’s important to stay safe. Before you go, make sure you have a map of the area and know the trail conditions (rain, snow, landslides, … can make the trail conditions too rough). Bring enough water and snacks, and let someone know your plans, let them know where you’ll be hiking and when you are expected to get back. Wear appropriate clothing and footwear, and be aware of the weather forecast, you don’t want to be halfway a hike and it starts snowing, raining and hailing. It’s also a good idea to carry a first aid kit, and to know basic first aid.

That’s about it

Hiking is a great way to get outside, exercise, and enjoy nature. It can also be a way to challenge yourself, push your limits, and learn new things. One of the most rewarding aspects of hiking is the sense of adventure and discovery that comes with it.

Remember, getting lost while hiking can be a positive experience, it allows you to see new things and make unexpected discoveries. But, it’s also important to stay safe, make sure you have a map, know the trail conditions, bring enough water and snacks, and let someone know your plans. With the right attitude and preparation, hiking can be a wonderful and rewarding experience.

Have fun on your hiking trips. ;)
