Lost in a Forest Semoine

  2017-06-01  -    Voylin's Life

Just a random vlog

This is just a random vlog from my time in Semoine when I was there with my family in our mobile home. It was a nice relaxing trip (except for the kayaking in a river filled with greenery). I decided to go for a solo hike and tried to get lost to have some fun.

I’ve always liked hiking, but because of the speed at which I walk, my family can’t easily keep up so I go alone. My main reason for trying to get lost every time that I hike is because when you get lost it gives you the feeling of adventure, or at least most of the time. ^^"
