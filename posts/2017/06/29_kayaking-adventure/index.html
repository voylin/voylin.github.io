Kayaking Adventure - Semoine

  2017-06-29  -    Voylin's Life

Kayaking in Semoine

A long and hard adventure in a kayak, I felt so tired and I really thought I filmed more. I really like kayaking, but the river was in a very terrible shape with all the greenery.

I don’t plan on going Kayaking again soon after this hellish tour through a dense greenery packed river. This was everything except relaxing. This was probably the hardest exercise of my life. Would not want to do this again! Next time I’ll check the condition of the river before deciding to go Kayaking again hahah.
