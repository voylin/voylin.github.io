Kayaking Adventure - Semoine

  2017-06-29  -    Voylin's Life

Kayaking in Semoine

A long and hard adventure in a kayak, I felt so tired and I really thought I filmed more. I really like kayaking, but the river was in a very terrible shape with all the greenery.

Kayaking in Belgium

Belgium may not be the first country that comes to mind when thinking of kayaking destinations, but the country’s network of canals and rivers offer a unique and picturesque way to explore its cities and countryside. Whether you’re a seasoned kayaker or new to the sport, Belgium is a great place to try it out.

The canals of Brussels

One place which you may not think of to go kayaking is the capital city, Brussels. It is a great place to start your kayaking adventure. The city is crisscrossed by a network of canals, many of which are open to kayakers. Paddle through the historic center and take in the architecture, street art, and local culture. The peaceful, traffic-free canals offer a unique perspective on the city and a chance to escape the hustle and bustle of the streets.

I have never done it, but having looked up the possibilities, it actually sounds quite tempting to me. However, I prefer the more nature scenery when going kayaking and the roughness of the river.

The Ardennes

This is probably the most well known place in Belgium to go Kayaking, a lot of foreigners only know Belgium because of Brussels, Brugge and Antwerp, but there is a whole other side to Belgium filled with mountains (or big hills :p), rivers and a lot of wild nature.

My most favorite place to go to for kayaking is around La Roche. That area has a nice atmosphere around it and the river is nice and calm most of the time, so an easy relaxing, and enjoyable tour can be had there.

But after this one …

I don’t plan on going Kayaking again soon after this hellish tour through a dense greenery packed river. This was everything except for relaxing. This was the hardest exercise of mey life. Would not want to do this again, next time I’ll check the condition of the river first before departing on this kind of an adventure.
