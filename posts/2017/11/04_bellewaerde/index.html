Bellewaerde trip with a friend

  2017-11-07  -    Voylin's Life

Bellewaerde park

I really enjoyed going to this amusement park as a child and I have seen it change so much compared to the first time I was there. Still really enjoy going there and waiting lines are never really that long. If you want some nice attractions and a fun time without too much waiting, go here!

Family park

This park has always focused itself on families. They have some rollercoasters but it’s not their main focus. Their focus is more on animals and family attractions, nothing to wild so the whole family can enjoy their trip to Bellewaerde, they even have a stunt and dive show! That show had not been running for a long time, but the last couple of years it has been going again. I remembered visiting the park as a kid and I was always wondering what those constructions were for, I guess it was too expensive to keep the show running at some point so they put it on a break?

Bad decisions

One of their most popular attractions was a simple boat tour inside of a building showing the life of pirates. It was quite old and maybe needed some work, but they decided to replace it by a rollercoaster instead. I guess maintenance became too high but I was not the only person upset by this.

Nostalgic

I went to this park more then I can remember, sometimes alone, other times with family or friends, sometimes with school as well. But I always had a nice time. I haven’t been there since I made this video (now 2023). Thinking back of all those times makes me really miss my childhood and makes me feel nostalgic.

I can even remember the bad fishy smell from the water attractions and the smells certain attractions had for some reason. Still can not explain how some places in the park got such a typical smell hahaha for some reason I remember each area of that park by a certain smell, which is quite odd as my sense of smell has only been going down recently ^^"

All by all, I really miss that park as it was small and easy to travel to. It was also not that busy most of the time so maximum waiting time was something like 10 minutes for each attraction during a busy summer day. hahahah I remember complaining about that, now that I went to USJ in Japan I don’t know what I was complaining about, waiting times in USJ easily exceed 30 minutes and even 1 hour …
