Kyoto Day trip - Empty Kyoto + Filming new outro

  2021-06-02  -    Voylin's Life, Our Life

My day trip in Kyoto

I’ve always liked Kyoto a lot and for this day trip we went to a part of Kyoto that I hadn’t been to before to eat in a restaurant located above a river. It feels very much aimed at tourists but it gave a relaxing atmosphere. The temperature was just right, not too many people and it was an overall relaxing day.

At this time Kyoto was still quite empty because of foreigners not being allowed back into Japan yet, so I was also able to film my new outro in Fushimi Inari. I had been planning to film my new outro there for some time but it’s quite difficult to find that place empty for long enough to do a shoot like that. We were quite lucky. Enjoy this video of a day trip in our life living in Japan! ;)
