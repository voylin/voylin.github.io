All JLPT N3 Grammar - Quick Japanese

A nice “short” little video for people studying for the JLPT N3 test. In this mini-series, I will help everybody who is going to take the JLPT exams by providing a video with the grammar rules together with an example of easy studying/understanding.

MISTAKES IN THE VIDEO: 35: The Noun and いAdj conjugation are swapped. – Thanks to reddit user enpitsu89 for pointing this out ;)
