Japanese Insect Museum - Closeby Osaka/Kobe

  2021-04-10  -    Voylin's Life, Travel Advice

Insect museum

I like many things, electronics, animals, nature, programming, making videos, diy, games and of course insects. I don’t know where my interest for insects came from as my entire family really dislikes them. But I find them very fascinating and interesting. They have such a unique way of living and handling situations. So I was quite happy to go to this insect museum, even though that the people I went with don’t really like insects hahah.
