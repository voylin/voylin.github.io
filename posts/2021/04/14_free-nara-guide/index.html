Free Online Guide Through Nara Japan

  2021-04-14  -    Voylin's Life, Travel Advice

Free guides in Nara Japan (online)

Want to travel in Japan, but there’s a pandemic going on? Go on an online tour with Nara Student Guides to see a small but beautiful part of Japan. ^-^

Link: https://narastudentguide.org/

I went on on of their normal guides when I visited Japan for the first time and it’s one of the best ways to learn more about Japan and it’s history in a quick, interesting and engaging way whilst strolling through the city of nara.
