A stroll through Osaka and Nara light festival

  2016-12-17  -    Voylin's Life

Nara Light festival

A yearly festival which is really worth going to. It basically feels like you are entering Disneyland but then without the attractions. I had an amazing time there with the people I’ve met in the hostel there in Nara and the life lessons they gave me. Often in these videos about my Big Adventure, I don’t really show or say why I was actually on that trip. It was to sort out my own life a little bit. That time in Nara with those people was a very big help for me and I am very grateful for the time we spend together.

Strolling through Osaka

This is probably one of my most favorite things to do. I really dislike busy areas, but in Osaka you can find so many places with almost no people, but with still a nice atmosphere. There is good food everywhere and there is enough to see behind every corner. It starts raining? Go underground as you can get anywhere without ever having to see daylight or feel the rain hahah.
