7kg Backpack Worldtrip - Winter edition

  2016-07-07  -    Voylin's Life, Travel Advice

In the video I showed how I prepared my bag to travel long-term in winter. It is colder so you need sweaters and warm clothes, those things can take up a lot of space. Still, I managed to keep the overall weight down to 7KG. I show how I did this in the video.

But why would you want to keep your backpack so light?

  1. Convenience and Mobility; (Carrying only one bag makes it possible to go to different places and to travel faster compared to with a suitcase)
  2. Cost Savings; (No paying needed for luggage when you have carry-on luggage)
  3. Simplicity and Minimalism; (Keep your life simple and mess free by carrying only necessary stuff)
  4. Improved Safety. (Only one bag to keep an eye on)
