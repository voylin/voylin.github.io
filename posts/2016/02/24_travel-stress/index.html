Travel stress for solo travel

First time traveling completely solo to Japan. Here is a playlist of all videos of this solo trip and the blog post: My playlist! My blog post!

Before big adventures I always get stressed and start thinking about all the ways certain things can go wrong to prepare myself for them. But especially when traveling to foreign countries where you haven’t been to before can really cause some worries.

But are these feelings of stress bad or good? Well, I actually like them. It’s the same as having feelings that you are excited! Can be uncomfortable at times, but it’s a part of the adventure you’re going on! :D
