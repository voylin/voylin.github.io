My Room - Belgium

  2016-06-23  -    Voylin's Life

My old room in Belgium

Since I moved out to live in Japan (2020), I have been missing my room as it was the place where I started with this youtube channel. I’ve had many fun, sad, depressing, excited and relaxing feelings in that room. We were living in that house since my 3 year of Junior high. Often the complaint came from me that the room was too small, but I was always able to make it work. It did not help of course that I wanted a double bed in that room, hahah what was I thinking??

All by all, I regret having chosen the color blue, but the color also made me more relaxed at times as my head can often go on a journey of its own.

Have I always had my own room?

As the oldest of 3, I was always lucky that I could have my own room, I liked the privacy and freedom to do whatever I want in my room without having to worry about my brothers changing things. I tried to keep my room clean and did a good job of that most of the time, however then there is our grandma. I love her a lot, but she always choose the wrong times to do room inspections.

My brothers had a worse time as they both did not care if their rooms were clean or not. They are working and over 20 now and they still have not figured out how to remove dust or how that magical sucking device works, a vacuum cleaner.

My last room in my parents house

The room in the video was my last room, from all rooms in that house I choose the smallest room, which came with some challenges like trying to fit a full sized bed inside of it hahah. But I choose the smallest one for a couple of reasons. The main reason was to give my brothers a bigger room and I was planning on starting to travel more after I started working anyway. Also a smaller room is easier to clean.

Size does matter

Having had such a tiny room made me more conscious about not buying useless crap. It made me think more about my purchase decisions and the way I put/displayed everything in my room. In that tiny space I had my desk/study area, a full sized bed, a giant closet and I even managed to create a ‘Background set’ for my youtube videos! It’s all about being creative with the limits that you have.

Limits

The more limits you have, the better as it forces you to be creative. Having lived in a very tiny apartment in Japan recently made me really consider to do many things differently, however some things I wasn’t going to give up, I really wanted a full sized desktop pc for my programming and gaming as I don’t feel comfortable with a laptop. When something breaks in a laptop it’s game over, compared to a pc, you just replace a part and you are good to go.

So it does not always mean that you have to limit yourself on everything, make compromises. Like in my tiny room in Belgium, I still wanted a full size bed no matter what. I am a big person and a single person bed was not giving me decent sleep.

We as humans can easily adjust ourself to any situation as long as we are open to it, so if you are moving to a tiny house or apartment, don’t worry and be creative. ;)
