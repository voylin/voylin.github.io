Streetfestival Belgium

  2016-06-15  -    Voylin's Life

What is a street festival?

In my neighborhood in Belgium where I used to live, we started having a meetup once a year to improve the overall relationship between the neighbors as our whole area had only 1 entry/exit street. This is one of our festivals which I recorded and wanted to share with you guys, enjoy!

Is this common?

No idea if this kind of a thing is common in Belgium, especially nowadays. It’s kind of a sad thing, I remember my parents getting along very well with our neighbors in all places where we lived, but it seems to be more and more a thing of the past as people prefer their own privacy and own time more then connecting with the people who live around us.

This is probably not everywhere the case, and I really do hope this is not going to be everywhere the case as well. But in recent years, there has been a growing trend of people becoming more and more distant from their neighbors in Belgium. This disconnect is being caused by a variety of factors, including the fast-paced nature of modern life, the rise of technology and social media, and a lack of community engagement.

Let’s talk about some of the reasons for this upward trend of becoming less social.

The fast-paced modern life

One of the main reasons people are becoming more distant from their neighbors is the fast-paced nature of modern life. People are working longer hours and have less free time to socialize and engage with their community. This means that there is less time for people to build relationships with their neighbors, and less opportunity for them to get to know each other.

Social media and technology

Another factor contributing to the disconnect between people and their neighbors is the rise of technology and social media. The internet and social media have made it easier for people to connect with others, but they have also made it easier for people to become isolated. Instead of going out and interacting with their neighbors, people are spending more time online and on their phones, which means that they are missing out on opportunities to build relationships with the people around them.

Lack of community engagement

A lack of community engagement is another reason people are becoming more distant from their neighbors. People are not participating in community events and activities, and are not joining clubs and organizations that bring people together. This means that people are not building relationships with their neighbors, and are missing out on opportunities to connect with the people around them.

My parents started this street festival, if they would not have started, we would not have become a neighborhood which actually knows each other. All it takes is 1 person or a couple of people to start making a change, even a small change like deciding to do something as a street festival could have a big effect.

And there is not that much planning involved, maybe a week or 2 of work, for benefits which far outweigh the time you put into it. For next year, other people will want to join in on the planning as well and you got a whole thing going now with multiple people and growing your event bigger and bigger, and connecting more and more people.

Conclusion

The disconnect between people and their neighbors in Belgium is a growing trend that is being caused by a variety of factors. The fast-paced nature of modern life, the rise of technology and social media, and a lack of community engagement are all contributing to this trend.

It’s important for people to make an effort to connect with their neighbors and build relationships with the people around them. This can be done by participating in community events and activities, joining clubs and organizations, and making an effort to interact with the people in your neighborhood. By connecting with your neighbors, you can create a stronger and more connected community, and improve the quality of life for everyone.

Having more social contact will make your mental health also better. It’s no coincidence that there are a lot more stress and burnout related issues nowadays compared to earlier times. Go out and open your mouth to people! ^o^/
