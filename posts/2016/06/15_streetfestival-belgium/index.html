Streetfestival Belgium

  2016-06-15  -    Voylin's Life

What is a street festival?

In my neighborhood in Belgium where I used to live, we started having a meetup once a year to improve the overall relationship between the neighbors as our whole area had only 1 entry/exit street. This is one of our festivals which I recorded and wanted to share with you guys, enjoy!

Is this common?

No idea if this kind of a thing is common in Belgium, especially nowadays. It’s kind of a sad thing, I remember my parents getting along very well with our neighbors in all places where we lived, but it seems to be more and more a thing of the past as people prefer their own privacy and own time more then connecting with the people who live around them.

This is probably not everywhere the case, but in recent years there has been a growing trend of people becoming more and more distant from their neighbors in Belgium. Having started this street festival made everybody in the neighborhood grow closer to each other. ^^
