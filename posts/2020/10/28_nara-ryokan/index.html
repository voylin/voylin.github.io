Staying at a Ryokan

Staying at a Ryokan is such a nice relaxing experience, although I must admit that the food is always too much and very difficult to eat. But it’s sooooo delicious as well. :D Enjoy the video in which we stay at Ryokan Mikasa in Nara, it really was a very nice stay and had a lot of fun.
