How to move to Japan - Guide, costs and how to?

  2020-10-19  -    Voylin's Life, Travel Advice

How to move to Japan

So moving to Japan can be quite expensive, but how much exactly? In this video, I will talk from the very beginning, all the way until receiving my driver’s license, which was the last step for me.

This is a plan that will guide you through all the steps of moving abroad to Japan, in 6 months. First 3 months are the planning and paperwork in your own country, the last 3 months are how to do things in Japan so you can actually start living there without worrying too much. I go into detail about how to make it go as smooth as possible for everybody who wants to go abroad and live in Japan. ;)

This video took me a very long time to make, a lot of work and effort went into making this so I hope it’s helpful to you! Enjoy! ;)
