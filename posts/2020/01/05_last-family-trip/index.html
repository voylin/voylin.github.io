Last family trip before moving - Pairi Daiza

  2020-01-05  -    Voylin's Life, Our Life

Pairi Daiza or Paradisio?

The park used to be called Paradisio, now it’s Pairi Daisa but I have been coming here since I was small so it’s difficult to change it in my mind when I talk about this park. I like animals a lot and this park also has such nice theming. It really has such a nice and relaxing atmosphere around it that I could easily walk there a whole day and not be tired.

If you ever come to Belgium, you have to go to this park! I don’t know if it’s the best in Europe, but I always call it the best in Europa hahah. The staff working there are also very friendly and the restaurants with Buffet are amazing!!

Last family trip

This is my last family trip before I go to Japan to live there. My life will change a lot after getting married, moving to Japan and when I start working there (if I can even find a job ^^"). I was never really a very social person, so the best contact with people are with my family. I will miss them, but this is also a great adventure for me, who can say that they will live in a foreign country, 16 hours away by plane, in a country where the language is still mostly unknown to you?

I hope I can visit my family in some years, and I really hope I can show my family this place which I fell in love with.
