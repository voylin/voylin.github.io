New year goals for 2020! - No JLPT anymore

  2020-01-02  -    Voylin's Life

New year goals!

Everybody knows new year goals and almost every breaks their new years resolutions. I wonder if I’ll break mine again this year or not. ^^" Luckily I have been starting to make my new years resolutions more and more realistic as I got older. There’s no point in setting goals which you deep down know, you can’t achieve.

I’m not saying that you should not aim high, but know what is possible for you to do. I wish I could say that I’ll reach the JLPT N1 by the end of the year. But time and energy won’t allow me to actually achieve those goals so what’s the point in lying to myself. It’s better to set a goal of wanting to study a certain amount instead.

Good luck with your new years resolutions! ;)
