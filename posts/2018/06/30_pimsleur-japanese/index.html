For people who want the level 1 Japanese series in CD’s: Pimsleur Japanese Level 1

Pimsleur Japanese Review

A review about Pimsleur, which I personally find one of the best audio lessons for helping with learning Japanese. But the price tag is unreasonable and hard to justify when there are other audio lessons for free like NHK.

NHK AUDIO LESSONS https://www.nhk.or.jp/lesson/english/

This is a nice resource, however I do not support the practice of NHK to act like a mafia in Japan forcing people to pay because they own a television and therefore can wat NHK so they have to pay. This is really crazy in my opinion and one of the first times I got in contact with such a mafia type person who tried to force me to sign that contract was extremely racist towards me.
