The secret of learning Japanese

Basically, there is not really any secret, there are some tips I could give you, but that’s about it. If there would really be an easy way to learn this language then most otakus and other people would already be fluent in Japanese.

But here are some tips in case you don’t want to watch the video! :p

  • Motivation;
  • Setting realistic goals;
  • Celebrating small wins;
  • Finding meaningful reasons to learn;
  • Surrounding yourself with support (if possible).
