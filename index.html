About me

Who am I?

I'm Voylin, 28 years old, born and raised in Belgium and now living in Japan together with my wife and 1 year old kid. Voylin is my nickname as I prefer not to use my real name online for privacy (even though I share quite a large part online on my youtube channel).

Programming has been a passion of me since I got my first pc when I was 8/9 years old. Whilst playing games I would always try to find ways to discover how those games were made. Figuring out how things work has been a part of my life for as long as I can remember, as a kid I would open almost anything which I didn't understand to see if I could understand by looking and tinkering.
