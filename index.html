About Me

I'm Voylin, a Belgian guy who moved to Japan in 2020 and became a solo developer in April 2023 from which I started mainly working on my open-source video editor. I have also released some other software already, but my video editor is still my priority. I'm also a full time stay-at-home dad and as a way to get some amount of income I'm doing YouTube on my 3 channels.

Projects

Here are some of the projects I've been working on. My main focus is GoZen, an open-source video editor built with Godot. But occasionally I find the time to work on other projects as well.
GoZen Video Editor

Open-source video editor built with Godot. In active development for 2 years, recently entered alpha stage.

GDE GoZen

An open-source GDExtension to provide video playback in Godot by using FFmpeg.

Teledot

An open-source teleprompter for your phone which you control through your computer.

An open-source audio recorder which was made in Godot as part of my tutorial series 'From zero to publishing'.
